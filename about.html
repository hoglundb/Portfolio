Brian Hoglund

I am a software developer with a passion for computer graphics and game developement. I am part of a small indie dev team and we are currently working on our first game title, which we hope to release on Steam sometime in the next year or so. I have also recently taken up a interest in augmented and mixed reality, and I am exited to see the transformation that this technology is making on the engineering industry.

I earned bachelor's degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science form Oregon State University in 2016 and 2020 respectivly. I started out my professional career as a mathematics instructor, but my love for these subjects ultimately lead me to pursue a career in software development. In addition to my work as an indie game developer, I am currently employed as a civilan for the Naval Undersea Warefare Center where I am privileged to have the opportunity to use my skills to support the Warefighter.
